Our History

Pamantasang Augernaut (AgU) was originally established as a Greek-run school with a strong emphasis on science, philosophy, and mathematics. Over the decades, the university evolved, embracing a Catholic and Vincentian identity under the Congregation of the Mission. This transformation strengthened its mission to provide high-quality education that is both accessible and affordable to students from all walks of life.

Our Mission & Vision

At Augernaut University, we strive to cultivate an academic environment that fosters critical thinking, ethical leadership, and lifelong learning. We believe in shaping individuals who are not only academically competent but also socially responsible, embodying the values of service and compassion.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution producing graduates who are innovative, principled, and prepared to contribute to society.

Our Mission

To provide quality, values-driven education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, professional competence, and a sense of social justice.
